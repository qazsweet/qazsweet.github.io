Goodbye Enschede

Enschede, a small city of the Netherlands, which I spend eighteen months for master study.

These people are really hard-working, even some of them do not think so. Whenever and wherever they want, they think and work, not only for job, but for interests with passion. It sometimes like craaazy. It confuses me sometime that if there should be a directed and consistant flag for me. I like so many things, which is hard to decide to focus.

Although there are always homeworks and exams, there are a lot trips. Nearly every break, a landmark is lighted. Italy for the first Christmas break, Bulgaria for spring break, Switzerland for summer break, Germany and Austra for study trip. Utrecht, Delft, Cologne, Rijsenhout, Natinaal Park De Hoge Veluwe, Henglo, Gronau, Rotterdam, Amsterdam, Den Haag, Brussel for weekends. And Spain and Portugal for graduation trip.

DATE: 31/03/2019

LOCATION: Enschede
